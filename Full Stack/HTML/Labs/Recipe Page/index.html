Indonesian-Style Stir-Fried Noodles

Indonesian Styled Noodles!!!

Ingredients

  • 4 Eggs
  • 400g Chow Mein Noodles
  • 2 Sweet Bell Peppers
  • 340g Coleslaw Cabbage Mix
  • 3 tablespoons Soy Sauce
  • 2 tablespoons Sweet Soy Sauce (Kecap Manis)
  • 2 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon Vegetable Oil
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • Chopped Green Onions (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Cook the chow mein noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan or wok, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add the minced garlic and stir-fry for 30 seconds.
  3. Add the bell peppers and coleslaw mix. Stir-fry for about 3â€“4 minutes until slightly tender.
  4. Push the vegetables to the side of the pan and crack the eggs into the empty space. Scramble and cook until set.
  5. Add the cooked noodles into the pan. Pour in the soy sauce and sweet soy sauce. Toss everything together until well combined and heated through.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Garnish with chopped green onions if desired.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
