Overview

Soldier: 76 is a versatile damage hero with a focus on mobility, sustained fire, and self-healing. His abilities allow him to deal damage at medium range while maintaining good positioning with his sprint and healing capabilities.

  • Role: Damage
  • Health: 200
Passive

As a damage hero, Soldier: 76 benefits from the passive ability that all damage characters share: increased movement speed after eliminations. This helps him chase down enemies and reposition quickly.

  • Passive: Damage role speed boost (after eliminations)
Heavy Pulse Rifle

The Heavy Pulse Rifle is Soldier: 76's primary weapon. It fires in fully automatic mode, with decent accuracy and damage over mid to long-range. It's perfect for consistent damage output while staying mobile.

  • Damage per shot: 18
  • Rate of fire: 9 rounds per second
  • Ammo: 30 rounds
  • Reload time: 1.5 seconds
Helix Rockets

Helix Rockets are a secondary fire option for Soldier: 76, launching a small burst of rockets that explode on impact. These rockets deal significant burst damage and are great for finishing off enemies or applying pressure in close combat.

  • Damage: 120 (direct hit)
  • Cool down: 6 seconds
  • Area-of-effect damage: 80
Sprint

Sprint allows Soldier: 76 to run at an increased speed, making him highly mobile. He can use Sprint to quickly get into position, escape danger, or chase down fleeing enemies. This ability has no cooldown, allowing him to use it freely.

  • Increases movement speed by 50%
Biotic Field

Soldier: 76 deploys a Biotic Field, which creates an area that heals him and any allies within its range. This ability provides crucial sustainability, making Soldier: 76 a durable threat in prolonged fights.

  • Healing: 40 health per second
  • Duration: 5 seconds
  • Cooldown: 15 seconds
Tactical Visor

Tactical Visor is Soldier: 76's ultimate ability. When activated, it automatically locks on to enemies in his line of sight, allowing him to fire his weapon without missing. This is particularly powerful in team fights where precision and quick eliminations are key.

  • Duration: 6 seconds
  • Automatically aims at enemies in line of sight
  • Can still reload and use abilities while active
Play Style

Soldier: 76 is best played as a versatile DPS who can deal consistent damage, heal himself and teammates, and reposition quickly. He's effective in both mid-range engagements and flanking strategies. Use Sprint to control your positioning, Biotic Field for sustain, and Helix Rockets for burst damage. Tactical Visor should be activated when you have a clear line of sight on multiple enemies for maximum value.

  • Mid-range damage dealer
  • Focus on positioning and sustain
  • Great for beginners and experienced players alike
