Overview

Armed with cutting-edge weaponry, including an experimental pulse rifle that’s capable of firing spirals of high-powered Helix Rockets, Soldier: 76 has the speed and support know-how of a highly trained warrior.

Passive
  • Damaging an enemy temporarily reduces their healing received.
  • Duration: 2 seconds
  • Reduces the target's healing received by 20%.
Heavy Pulse Rifle

Soldier 76's Weapon the Heavy Pulse Rifle

  • Damage: 19 – 5.7
  • Falloff range: 30 – 50 meters
  • Headshot: ✓
  • Rate of fire: 9 rounds per second
  • Ammo: 30
  • Reload time: 1.5-seconds animation
  • Projectile radius: 0.08 meters

Adjust your rate you fire depending on the range you're fighting at. Use fully automatic fire when fighting up close and at medium range, and fire in three-round bursts at long range, exploiting the Heavy Pulse Rifle's perfect accuracy for its first 3 shots in a burst. (Spread has been replaced with vertical recoil, so the accuracy remains pin-point no matter how many shots you take.)

Helix Rockets

Soldier 76's Weapon the Heavy Pulse Rifle

  • Damage: 40 (direct hit)
    - 80 – 40 (splash, enemy)
    - 40 – 20 (splash, self)
  • Headshot: ✕
  • Duration: 0.5 seconds recovery for primary
  • Area of effect: 3 meter radius
  • Move. speed: +4 meters per second (enemy after direct hit)
  • Projectile speed: 50 meters per second
  • Projectile radius: 0.35 meters

- Helix Rockets can be used to deal burst damage, killing an enemy before they can escape or use an ability.

- If you're unsure of your aim, or are against an evasive opponent, fire Helix Rockets at your enemy's feet instead of directly at them. This will ensure that the explosion damages them, even if you can't directly hit them with the rockets.

- Helix Rockets self damage can kill you if your health is too low.

Sprint

Soldier 76's Sprint

  • Duration: ∞
  • Move. speed: +50%

- Whether he needs to evade a firefight or get back into one, Soldier: 76 can rush ahead in a burst of speed. His sprint ends if he takes an action other than charging forward.

- The activation of the ability can be customized between holding the button and toggling with a single press.

- After Sprint is cancelled, there is a 0.3 second recovery period where Soldier: 76 cannot use primary or secondary fire.

Biotic Field

Soldier 76's Weapon the Heavy Pulse Rifle

  • Healing: 40 per second
  • Cast time: 0.54 seconds (cannot be canceled by enemy)
  • Duration: 5 seconds
  • Area of effect: 4.5 meter radius

- Cannot be destroyed.

- Can be placed on and lifted by Petal Platform Icon Petal Platform if the latter was deployed first.

- Healing is only applied to targets within line of sight of the biotic emitter, ignoring enemy barriers.

Tactical Visor

Soldier 76's Weapon the Heavy Pulse Rifle

  • Headshot: ✓
  • Reload time: 0.65 seconds
  • Cast time: 1.2 seconds
  • Duration: 6 seconds

- Activation of Tactical Visor instantly replenishes Icon-ability.hpr Heavy Pulse Rifle's ammo.

- Damage falloff still applies

- Soldier: 76 can headshot only if the shot would have been a headshot without Tactical Visor. Additionally, it is possible to land headshots without aiming at the head during Zenyatta's animation when he fires an orb volley.

- Soldier: 76 will only lock on to a target if their center of mass is visible and not behind an enemy barrier

- The aim lock does not apply to Helix Rockets.
